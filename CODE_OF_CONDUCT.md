# Code of Conduct

All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to follow a standard of respectful and inclusive behavior.

Expected behavior:
- Be welcoming and considerate to others.
- Use inclusive language and avoid derogatory comments.
- Assume good faith and be constructive when giving feedback.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, hateful language, or personal attacks.
- Posting sexually explicit content or violent threats.
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

Reporting unacceptable behavior:

Consequences:
Project maintainers may take action such as removing content, blocking users, or contacting hosting providers depending on the severity of the behavior.
